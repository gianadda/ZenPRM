    Release 1.2.3 - 09/01/2016

    Release 1.2.3

    Howdy ZenPRM users!

    This week we bring you another round of bug fixes and some enhancements both big and small.

    1)      Participants in projects now have the ability to delete files in their personal repositories.

    2)      We’ve added a handy button to the dashboard that allows you the customer to toggle between all Key Performance Measures and only those are set to appear on the Dashboard.  This gives you a quick glimpse at everything without having to navigate away from your dashboards.

     

    3)      You can now change the resource type directly from the resource profile. This change is for private to public conversions and if you are converting a provider you must provide an NPI number to continue.

    4)      Resources are now prevented from being able to delete an email address. If you or a resource needs to change the email associated with ZenPRM, they can add an additional email address and then can delete the original.  If a resource however has used a social media account to log into ZenPRM, they will not be able to adjust their email address. If the email address associated with the social media account changes this will reflect in ZenPRM.

    5)      We have locked in the NPI numbers, once one is set with a resource it cannot be edited.  If you believe an NPI number is incorrect, please reach out to our help team to assist you. They can be reached at zensupport@ahealthtech.com

    6)      We have also updated our resource exports to include the resource first name, last name, middle name and organization name.

    7)      And finally, the piece we’ve been most excited about this week, and in the spirit of giving you easy access to data, we’ve added Medicare Part D Prescriber Data and Physician Summaries as provided by CMS.gov in a simple, clean view for you to analyze and assess.  Here’s a quick peek at what these screens look like:
