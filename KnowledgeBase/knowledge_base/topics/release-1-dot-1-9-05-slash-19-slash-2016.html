    Release 1.1.9- 05/19/2016

    Howdy! 
    This week's release was a little smaller, we took some time to fix some minor bugs and ensure things were running great on the back end to give you the best experience you can get.  Here is what we've been up to:

    1) There is now a warning when you try to enter in an email address that is already in use on a profile.  We've also linked that profile in the warning message so you can see who that email belongs to. 

    2) We fixed a bug on the 'Key Performance Measures' ratio dials where currency was the numerator. Previously it was not displaying the appropriate range color, but we took care of that. 

    3) We have added browser requirements to the 'Login' and 'Registration' pages.  You can also view these requirements here.

    4) We've added a quick "Add to Network" link on the 'Global Resources' page when you click the check box next to the resources name. 

    5) We fixed a bug in "Bookmarked Projects" which were previously showing the wrong project list

    6) When exporting a project, Yes/No answers are no longer showing as True/False in your exported .csv file. 

    7) We have changed the way you delegate into an account. When you log in, you will no longer be taken to your personal profile page. If you are responsible for just one organization, when you log in with your personal account, you will  be taken right to your organizations dashboard. If you are responsible for more than one organization, after you log in, you will be taken to a page where you can select which account you would like to work under. You can still access your personal profile by clicking the round avatar in the bottom left hand corner. 

    If you have any further questions, please reach out to us at zensupport@ahealthtech.com.
