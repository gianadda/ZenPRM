    Release 1.1.17- 07/28/2016

    Hi ZenPRM users!  Here are the latest ZenPRM updates for this week. 

    1) We moved our messaging from the top right to bottom middle of your screen so it won't block any buttons or ruin your workflow. 

    2) In our Project Questions there was a bug when removing tags, previously it did not fully remove the tag, that has been corrected. 

    3) Project Import now allows for option types. What we mean is now if you upload a .csv file where participants have answered a question and selected multiple answers (see image below) you will be able to tell our import process to select those answers and you can choose your delimiter of choice.   

    Here we see the next step in the import process where you can map your answers and choose your delimiter. 

    4) we've added a new 'Ratings' scale as an option Project Questions and we have also added Horizontal Radio Buttons and checkboxes to better format your projects. 

    5) Finally, in 'My Resources' we have regrouped the order of our filters down down so that the most commonly used filters default to the top of the list. 

     

    And of course, you can always reach us at zensupport@ahealthtech.com
