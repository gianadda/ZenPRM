    Release 1.1.13 - 6/23/2016

    Tomorrow monring we will be launching version 1.1.13 of ZenPRM and as usual we've been busy working on enhancements and bug fixes to keep ZenPRM amazingly simple! 

    - The first thing you'll notice is our new Resource project modal.  This is a quick glimpse into your resources's demographics by clicking on their name. This is so you can have quick information to the resource your are reporting on without navigating away from the screen you are working on. 

     - Next, we've made it easier for you to view dig into your project questions and take a look at some Key Perfomance Measures related to that particular question. Take a look at this quick introductory video.  

    - We have also added two new filters to the My Resources section, you can now filter on email addresses and also filter on project due dates as well.

    -Finally, some small bug fixes and enhancements. 

    • Previously, if an organization was on a project, they would show up as unaffiliated instead of part of their own dial.  We've corrected this now so organizations are no longer 'unaffilitated'. 
    • We've added the resource name to the title tag for quick reference
    • Improved some UI features on Saved Searches and Opt-in/Opt-Out options on the profile screen. 

    As always, should you need any more assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our support team at zensupport@ahealthtech.com
