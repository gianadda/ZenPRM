    What can I find out from my 'Insights' page?

    The insights page in ZenPRM give you a snapshot of publically sourced information based on referral patterns and other Medicare Provider utilization and payment insights.

    ZenPRM can display referrals for Providers, Organizations, or Facilities with NPIs (National Provider Identifiers). A referral is counted when a single beneficiary visits one Provider (or Organization) and then visits another within a period of time. This information is provided by CMS under the Freedom of Information Act.

    Also, ZenPRM can display Medicare Provider utilization and payment insights, which summarize the utilization and payments for procedures, services, and prescription drugs provided to Medicare beneficiaries by specific inpatient and outpatient hospitals, physicians, and other suppliers. This information is provided by CMS for 2013 and 2014.
