    Release 1.1.8- 05/12/2016

    Welcome Back ZenPRM users! This week we've focused on some bug fixes and some minor tweaks, here's what you can expect: 

    Key Performance Measures (KPM): 

    -You can now track measures by ratio and you can also create dials who's answers are in currency. 

    -One enhancement we are really exicted about is the ability to really dig into each KPM by your organization and its hierarchy. Check out this quick video to see what we mean: 

     

    -The ranges you create will now always be visible on the dial hover. 

    -We've added the ability to add KPM dials to other areas of the system. The four areas KPM dials can exist are your organization's Dashboard, within the Organization Structure, on the Project Overiew and on a Resource's profile. 

    File Repository

    -We've added a "return to project" button now when choosing options from file repositories. 

    Tickets: 

    -We've added a successful save message to the ticket screens to highlight when items have been saved. 

    Project Import: 

    - We've made a couple changes to the workflow when adding a project via excisting spreadsheet. Our goal is to make that process amazingly simple! Please let us know what you think!

    As always, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at zensupport@ahealthtech.com if you need any further assistance.
