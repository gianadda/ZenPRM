    How do I go back and forth between my administrative delegate account and my personal account?

    When you log into ZenPRM you are defaulted to your personal profile page. To switch, or delegate into your organization's page click on the avatar on the bottom left side of your screen. It may look something like this: 

    From there, you will get a menu of options to sign out, change password, swap roles, and see profile. You will also see the option to view all your delegate accounts.
