    Release 1.1.12 - 6/16/2016

    Hello again ZenPRM users! 
    We've been spending some more time dotting proverbial I's and crossing T's! This week's release features one enhancement to our system and a handful of bug fixes that users such as yourself have reported. Here is what we have been up to: 

    First, the bug fixes. 

    1) In the 'My Resources' section of ZenPRM, under the demographics tab, the 'Top Payors' chart was not displaying information when you hovered over the chart.  We've corrected that so that on a mouse hover you'll see a quick peak that that data. 

    2) In an Organization 'Profile', under the 'Structure' tab we've made the resource names display as they do in the rest of the system (Last Name, First Name). 

    3) We corrected a bug where in the 'Edit Profile' screen,  system dates were being shown as DD/MM/YYYY.  We've standardized that with the rest of ZenPRM and all dates are now showing as MM/DD/YYYY

    4) Under the 'Project' section in the 'Details' tab, previously, once a project was created, if a user went back to changing the 'Project Manager' that change did 'Project Contact'.  We have corrected this bug so that the change will now correlate. 

    5) Another bug we fixed was in the 'Projects' seciton, under the Participants tab, optional text not being included in outbound email if you selected to email your participants.  We have remedied this and all optional text is included in the email message to participants. 

    6) Under 'My Resources' we have added a filter on the organization list to make it simplier to find the organization you need. 

    7) We have also added some icons to our ticket screen so you can quickly see what type of interaction is being recorded. 

    And finally, our system enhancement this week! 

    We've took a lot of consideration and time into updating how you add ranges to Key Performance Measure's dials.  We hope you find the new layout as simple and easy to use as we do!  You can take a look at our other knowledge base articles on how to set up measures here.
