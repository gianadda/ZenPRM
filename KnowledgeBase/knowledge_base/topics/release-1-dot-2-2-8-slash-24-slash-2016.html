    Release 1.2.2 - 8/24/2016

    Greetings!

    Another productive week over at ZenPRM headquarters and here is what we have created, updated and spruced up for you.

     

    1)      We care about the ‘end user’ experience. We are classifying ‘end users’ as the medical professionals and other general contacts that will be using ZenPRM to update their demographics, respond to projects and upload files. The new end user landing page now allows one to see a quick preview of their personal demographics with the option to edit this information easily, their ongoing projects and quick links to some helpful FAQs.

     

    2)       We’ve added a quick ‘Add Resource to a Project’ link from the resource profile that is viewable to customers of ZenPRM.

     

    3)      We’ve added a link to edit a resource’s profile directly from the resource modal to minimize the number of clicks you need to make.

    4)      There was one bug fix in this release which was incorrect data showing in the ‘My Resources/Project Export’ options. We’ve corrected this so the data is accurate.

    5)      And finally, we’ve added a separate bulk import specifically for private contacts.  From your Import Resources screen, you’ll see the button on the top right so you can change over to importing private resources.

    This import works similarly to the traditional import resources function with the exception that the resources will be categorized as private and viewable only by delegates of your customer organization.

     

    For more helpful tips and trick please visit our help site at https://zenprm.groovehq.com/help_center and please do not hesitate to reach out to us at

    zensupport@ahealthtech.com should you have any questions or concerns or just wish to say ‘Hello!’.
