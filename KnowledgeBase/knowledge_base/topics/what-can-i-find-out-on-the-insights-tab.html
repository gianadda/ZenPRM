    What can I find out on the Insights Tab?

    The insights tab found on your resources profiles is intended to give you the ability to dig into the publically available CMS data.  CMS data can be daunting to sort through so it is our intent to give you that information in simple layout where you can see the public data available on your providers. 

    Currently, the insights tab holds information on:

    1) Physician referral data

    2) Medicare Part D Physicians Summary where you can see beneficiary demographics breakdowns, medical conditions treated and a financial summary

    3) Physician billing details as organized by HCPCS code: 

    4) Medicare Part D Prescriber Summaries which includes brand vs generic midications, drug costs and prescription drug plans. 

    5) And finally, Medicare Part D Prescriber Details.  This section includes a breakdown by drug supplied. 

     

    More details on all of these public data sets can be found at www.cms.gov
